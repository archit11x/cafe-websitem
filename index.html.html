Our Story

Born from a
love of slowness

Ember & Ground was born in 2018 from a single belief — that great coffee deserves time. We source micro-lot beans from Karnataka, Coorg, and Araku Valley, roast them in small batches, and brew every cup with deliberate care.

Our café in Indore is a refuge — warm amber light, the hum of a hand grinder, and the scent of something slow-roasted and honest. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a daily regular, you'll find your cup waiting just how you like it.
